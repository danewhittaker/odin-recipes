Straberry Banana Smoothie

Pineapple adds a bit of tropical flavor to this classic strawberry banana smoothie. Use frozen fruit for a frostier smoothie.

Ingredients

  • Banana
  • Fresh Strawberries
  • Almond Milk
  • Fresh Pineapple

Directions

  1. Have Blender Clean and Ready

    2. Puree all ingredients until smooth

  2. Serve Cold in Cup with Straw
